5 Seconds of Summer (5SOS) are a 4 man pop rock band originating from Australia, Sydney, New South Wales formed in 2011. The members consists of Luke Hemmings, Ashton Irwin, Calum Hood and Michael Clifford. They started out their careers through YouTube just by posting cover videos of themselves and eventually got recognised by other famous bands such as One direction who invited 5sos to tour with them in their album Take me home. Since then, they have produced 3 studio albums. 1 in early 2014 where they released their first album "She looks so perfect" where it reached number 1 both in Australia and the UK. Their second album was called "5 seconds of summer" where it reached number 1 in 11 countries. Finally after a lengthy break from the music industry, they released their third album called "Youngblood" in late 2018 which has proven to be big hit becoming their third album to top the charts in their home country. Furthermore especially with their single hit"Youngblood" becoming their highest charting single to date on the US billboard hot 100. The band has had to make changes to the type of music they make from previous more upbeat tunes to slower and more mature.

Luke Hemmings born July 16 1996, Luke is the lead singer of the band as well as being the rhythm guitarist

Michael Clifford born November 20 1995, Michael is the bands main guitarist and a singer-songwriter

Ashton Irwin born July 7 1994 Ashton is the bands drummer and a vocalist

Calum Hood born January 25 1996, Calum is the bassist of the band as well as being the vocalist
